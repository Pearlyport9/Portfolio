BIZCORE Consulting

A multi-page consulting website for a Lagos-based finance, market research, and data analytics consultancy, designed to build credibility and generate client leads.

Problem

BIZCORE had no online presence, no way to be found or vetted, and no consistent way to explain five distinct service lines to visitors with very different levels of financial literacy.

Prospective clients had nothing to reference before a first call: no past work to review, no service offerings to compare, no way to gauge credibility against other consultancies.

The sales pipeline relied entirely on referrals and cold outreach instead of inbound trust.

Solution / Process

Designed and built solo with a clean, corporate visual direction: geometric growth and financial imagery to reinforce trust and authority.

Structured around five core services: Financial Planning and FP&A, Market Research and Business Strategy, Data Analytics and Performance Intelligence, Investment Advisory and Pitch Deck Creation, and Business Development Strategy.

Hero built around the brand slogan "Structured Finance. Sustainable Growth." with a "Book a Consultation" CTA repeated across every page.

Features include a hero slider, bento-grid services layout, a blog with easy non-technical content updates, and an admin dashboard.

Designed for lead generation as the core goal: consultation request form, CTA on every page, prominent social and contact links.

Outcome

Delivered as a complete client project. BIZCORE Consulting is live, presenting its services and generating consultation leads across its target audience of founders, SMEs, and investors.
