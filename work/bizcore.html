BIZCORE Consulting

A multi-page consulting website for a Lagos-based finance, market research, and data analytics consultancy, designed to build credibility and generate client leads.

Hero video — TO FILL

Problem

BIZCORE needed to showcase its expertise across finance, market research, and data analytics to a mixed audience of startup founders, SME owners, investors, and business professionals — while presenting services clearly enough to convert visitors into consultation bookings, and credibly enough to support investor-facing branding.

Solution / Process

  • Designed and built solo with a clean, minimalist, corporate visual direction — geometric growth and financial imagery to reinforce trust and authority.
  • Structured the site around BIZCORE's five core services: Financial Planning & FP&A, Market Research & Business Strategy, Data Analytics & Performance Intelligence, Investment Advisory/Pitch Deck Creation, and Business Development Strategy.
  • Designed a hero around the brand slogan "Structured Finance. Sustainable Growth." with a clear "Book a Consultation" CTA repeated across pages.
  • Built features: hero slider, bento-grid services layout, blog with easy non-technical content updates, and an admin dashboard.
  • Designed for lead generation as the core functional goal — consultation request form, CTA buttons on every page, social/contact links.

Outcome

Delivered as a complete client project. BIZCORE Consulting is live, presenting its services and generating consultation leads across its target audience of founders, SMEs, and investors.
