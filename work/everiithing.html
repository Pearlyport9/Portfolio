Everiithing

Nigeria's first fully managed, verification-first marketplace for home and business service providers, designed and built end-to-end, from concept through full-stack deployment.

Problem

Finding a reliable artisan in Nigeria is a persistent everyday frustration. Customers face no reliable vetting (WhatsApp/Instagram are flooded with self-promoted, unverifiable providers), poor workmanship with no accountability, inflated and opaque pricing negotiated ad hoc, frequent no-shows, and zero formal recourse when things go wrong. On the other side, skilled artisans are invisible: buried under noise from unqualified competitors, with no way to build a verifiable professional identity, access consistent job leads, or grow a steady income pipeline. Both sides are stuck navigating a market with no trust infrastructure.

Solution / Process

  • Designed and built solo as a fully managed marketplace: the platform controls pricing, quality, and customer experience end-to-end to close the trust gap.
  • Built a 7-stage provider verification pipeline (document submission, NIN identity match, reference calls, guarantor submission, in-person interview, practical skills test, platform onboarding) as the core trust differentiator.
  • Designed flat-rate, transparent pricing across 7 service categories (plumbing, electrical, AC, generator/inverter, painting, deep cleaning, carpentry): no hidden fees, no negotiation.
  • Designed the full booking-to-payout flow: browse verified providers → instant booking → escrow payment → job completion confirmation → funds released to provider → rating/review.
  • Designed and built three connected surfaces: customer-facing booking app, provider dashboard (job notifications, earnings tracker), and an internal admin/ops panel (verification queue, dispute resolution, pricing management).
  • MVP scoped deliberately: excluded subscription tiers, live chat, GPS tracking, and native mobile apps from v1 to ship a focused Lagos pilot first.

Outcome

Shipped and soft-launched to early testers. Everiithing is live and actively onboarding its first customers and verified service providers across Lagos.
