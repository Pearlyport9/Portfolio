Ceembee Footies

A clean, minimal e-commerce site for a handmade leather shoes and slides brand: browse, pick your style, and checkout.

Problem

Ceembee Footies needed a simple, focused online storefront that let the product do the talking: handmade leather shoes and slides that needed to be seen clearly, selected easily, and purchased without friction. No complex flows, no unnecessary pages, just a clean shopping experience that matched the craftsmanship of the product. Handmade leather goods also don't translate well to generic marketplace listings, where product photos compete with mass-produced alternatives and the craftsmanship behind each pair is easy to overlook. The brand needed a storefront it fully controlled, from photography to checkout, to present that quality on its own terms. Built and maintained solo, the site also had to stay lightweight and easy to update without needing a developer for every new product drop or price change.

Solution / Process

  • Designed and built solo as a straightforward e-commerce experience, prioritizing a fast launch over feature breadth.
  • Core flow: landing page → product listing → product detail → cart → checkout, kept short to minimize drop-off.
  • Organized the catalog by style and category (shoes vs. slides) so customers could browse and find their size with minimal clicks.
  • Design focused on product photography as the hero element: large visuals, minimal UI clutter, letting the craftsmanship of each pair carry the page.
  • Built mobile-first, since most of the brand's traffic arrives through Instagram and other social links.
  • Structured content and pricing to be easy to update solo: new drops and price changes don't require touching code.

Outcome

Live and fully operational. Ceembee Footies is accepting orders, with a clean shopping experience that puts the handmade product front and centre.
