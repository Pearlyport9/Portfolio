Ceembee Footies

A clean, minimal e-commerce site for a handmade leather shoes and slides brand — browse, pick your style, and checkout.

Hero image — TO FILL

Problem

Ceembee Footies needed a simple, focused online storefront that let the product do the talking — handmade leather shoes and slides that needed to be seen clearly, selected easily, and purchased without friction. No complex flows, no unnecessary pages — just a clean shopping experience that matched the craftsmanship of the product.

Solution / Process

  • Designed and built solo as a straightforward e-commerce experience.
  • Core flow: landing page → product listing → product detail → cart → checkout.
  • Design focused on product photography as the hero element — large visuals, minimal UI clutter.

Outcome

Live and fully operational. Ceembee Footies is accepting orders, with a clean shopping experience that puts the handmade product front and centre.
