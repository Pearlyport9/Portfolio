Mikhiyah

Website for a mental wellness and community support organization.

Hero video — TO FILL

Problem

Mikhiyah needed a warm, accessible website that reflected its mission of mental wellness and community support — a digital space that felt as safe and intentional as the organisation itself.

Solution / Process

  • Part of the collaborative team responsible for designing the UI interface.
  • Web copy sourced from client-provided content.
  • Resources page: card-based grid (6 cards, two rows of three).
  • Split-screen Guinea section with 3 feature boxes.
  • Two-section Mindfulness / Faith / Spirituality layout.
  • Hero: "Tools for Healing, Hope, and Well-Being".

Client brand palette (specific to this project, not the portfolio's own design system): Cream White #F8F5F0, Olive Green #687F3C, Soft Gold #DAB96B, Terracotta #C96A3D.

Outcome

Delivered to the client and currently live at mikhiyah.org. The site serves the Mikhiyah community with accessible mental wellness resources, tools, and support content.
